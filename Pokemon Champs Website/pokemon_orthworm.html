PALDEA'S STRONGEST TEAM

ORTHWORM

Orthworm is a sturdy and dependable steel-type pokémon that's effective on defense. Even though as a steel pokémon it would typically be weak to ground-type moves, Orthworm has the Earth Eater ability which restores their health if hit by a ground type move. Additionally, Orthworm is also immune to poision-type moves, and most other types only do half damage against it. It's capability to learn ground and rock-type moves as well makes Orthworm a well-rounded choice for your team.

Current Moves

  • Dig (ground-type)
  • Smack Down (rock-type)
  • Bulldoze (ground-type)
  • Iron Head (steel-type)

Effective Against:

  • Ice-types
  • Fairy-types
  • Dragon-types
  • Rock-types

Weak Against

  • Fire-types
  • Fighting-types
  • Ground-types
