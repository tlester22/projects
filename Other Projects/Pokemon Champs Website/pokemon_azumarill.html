PALDEA'S STRONGEST TEAM

AZUMARILL

Azuramill is a hybrid water/fairy-type pokémon that provides top-notch defense and support in battle. On top of it's high HP and already short list of weaknesses, Azumarill's ability Thick Fat halves the damage they take from fire and ice-type moves. Azumarill's trump card is its immunity to dragon type moves, which are notariously widely effective.

Current Moves

  • Play Rough (fairy-type)
  • Rollout (rock-type)
  • Bubble Beam (water-type)
  • Rain Dance (water-type)

Effective Against:

  • Water-types
  • Fire-types
  • Ice-types
  • Fighting-types
  • Bug-types
  • Dark-types
  • Dragon-types

Weak Against

  • Electric-types
  • Grass-types
  • Poison-types
