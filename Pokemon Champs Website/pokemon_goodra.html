PALDEA'S STRONGEST TEAM

GOODRA

Goodra is a powerful dragon-type pokémon whose short list of weaknesses make it a reliable choice in battle. Not to mention, its capability to learn water-type and poison-type moves gives you three strong type options to work with when designing your attack strategy. With their ability Hydration, rain will cure any status conditions they incur in battle.

Current Moves

  • Sludge Bomb (poison-type)
  • Dragon Breath (dragon-type)
  • Dragon Pulse (dragon-type)
  • Acid Spray (poison-type)

Effective Against:

  • Water-types
  • Fire-types
  • Grass-types
  • Electric-types

Weak Against

  • Ice-types
  • Dragon-types
  • Fairy-types
