PALDEA'S STRONGEST TEAM

MEOWSCARADA

Meowscarada is a grass-type, offense-oriented pokémon. It's ability Overgrowth powers up their grass type moves when the pokémon's HP is low. It makes sure to pack a punch before retreating for healing! Moreover, Meowscarada is capable of learning dark-type moves, psychic-type moves and bug-types moves. This makes it a versatile choice as a starter.

Current Moves

  • Night Slash (dark-type)
  • Flower Trick (grass-type)
  • Trailblaze (grass-type)
  • Bite (dark-type)

Effective Against:

  • Water-types
  • Ground-types
  • Rock-types
  • Ghost-types
  • Psychic-types

Weak Against

  • Bug-types
  • Fire-types
  • Flying-types
  • Ice-types
  • Poison-types
  • Fairy-types
  • Fighting-types
