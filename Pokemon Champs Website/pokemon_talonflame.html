PALDEA'S STRONGEST TEAM

TALONFLAME

Talonflame is an offensive fire type pokémon that does a lot of damage! These pokémon are very speedy and will most likely hit first even against higher level pokémon. Their Flame Body ability can cause burns against opposing pokémon on contact, and if that doesn't work, hitting their opponent with a fire-type move will do the trick. Talonflame's fire-type/flying-type medley gives them many effective matchups.

Current Moves

  • Flame Charge(fire-type)
  • Aerial Ace (flying-type)
  • Acrobatics (flying-type)
  • Ember (fire-type)

Effective Against:

  • Bug-types
  • Fire-types
  • Steel-types
  • Ground-types
  • Steel-types
  • Fairy-types
  • Fighting-types

Weak Against

  • Water-types
  • Rock-types
  • Electric-types
